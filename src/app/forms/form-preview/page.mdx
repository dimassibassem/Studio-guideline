# Form Preview

After clicking on a form from the Forms list page, you will be taken to the Form preview page.

<Row>
    <Col>
        <ImagePreview
            src="/images/forms/form-preview.png" alt="Form preview"/>
    </Col>
    <Col>

        The Form preview page displays the form in more detail. You can see the form name, version, key and the
        date it was last modified.

        You can also see the form preview image, which shows the form fields .

        You can use the buttons at the top of the page to edit the form, view the form history, or delete the
        form.
    </Col>
</Row>
